Door Runner

Controls: Space to jump, P to pause, M to menu, Shift to dash, Enter or Z to vertical dash, and R to restart!
Platformer Mode: Use Arrow Keys or A/D to move left and right!
Reach the door to move to the next level, Avoid the spikes to stay alive! Land on top of walls, not the sides!
Now with speed boosts, jump pads, and crumbling platforms! You have 3 lives to make it as far as you can!
